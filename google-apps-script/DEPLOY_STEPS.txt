Google Apps Script Deployment Steps

1. Go to script.google.com and create a new project.
2. Paste the full contents of Code.gs.
3. Click Deploy > New deployment.
4. Select type: Web app.
5. Execute as: Me.
6. Who has access: Anyone.
7. Click Deploy and authorize permissions.
8. Copy the Web App URL.
9. Open js/contact_me.js and replace PASTE_YOUR_GOOGLE_APPS_SCRIPT_WEB_APP_URL_HERE with the Web App URL.
10. Upload/commit js/contact_me.js to your GitHub Pages repo.
