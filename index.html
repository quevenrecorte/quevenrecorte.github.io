About

I am known as an upbeat, self-motivated team player with several years of experience in electronic design, including schematic design, PCB layout, and prototyping for DIY projects and the consumer electronics industry. I have successfully developed designs from initial concepts and sketches through to fully functional prototypes.

My experience includes the design and development of development boards, RF circuits, breakout boards, and lighting products. I have built a strong track record of delivering consistent, high-quality results for the majority of my clients. If you’re looking for professional support for your electronic projects, feel free to reach out and we can discuss how I can help bring your ideas to life.
